# Venue Requirements Index

This directory now separates venue requirements into two sources because they have different evidence bases and update cycles.

## Canonical Split

1. Formal formatting and submission requirements:
- [formatting-requirements.md](formatting-requirements.md)

2. Empirical structural benchmarks from representative theses:
- [benchmark-requirements.md](benchmark-requirements.md)

## Why This Split Exists

- Formal requirements come from handbooks, templates, and submission rules.
- Benchmarks come from comparative analysis of successful theses in this venue.
- Keeping them separate avoids mixing "must comply" rules with "typically strong" patterns.

## Conflict Rule

If benchmark guidance conflicts with formal/template requirements, formal/template requirements always win.

## Review and Feedback Placement

- Review method and output format belong in:
  - [../.github/instructions/review.instructions.md](../.github/instructions/review.instructions.md)
- Structural benchmark criteria used by review belong in:
  - [benchmark-requirements.md](benchmark-requirements.md)
- Submission/compliance checks used by review belong in:
  - [formatting-requirements.md](formatting-requirements.md)

## Maintenance Rule

- Update [formatting-requirements.md](formatting-requirements.md) when official rules or templates change.
- Update [benchmark-requirements.md](benchmark-requirements.md) when benchmark sample size or quality basis changes.
- Keep this index short and stable.
